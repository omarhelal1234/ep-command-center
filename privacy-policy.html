Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This policy explains how the “Everything Psych Autopublish” application (the “App”) handles data. The App is a private, first-party tool used by the Everything Psych channel to publish our own videos to our own connected social accounts, including TikTok.

1. Data we access

When authorized, the App uses TikTok OAuth to obtain an access token and a refresh token for our own TikTok account, and reads basic account info (open id, display name) solely to confirm the correct account. It uses the video.upload scope to upload our videos.

2. How we use it

Tokens are used only to upload our content and are stored privately in our own local credentials file. We do not collect, process, or store personal data of any other TikTok users.

3. Sharing

We do not sell or share any data with third parties. Data is transmitted only between the App and TikTok’s official APIs.

4. Retention

Credentials are retained only while the App is in use and can be revoked at any time from the TikTok account’s connected-apps settings.
